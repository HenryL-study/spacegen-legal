Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information. Our primary goal is to provide AI-powered room editing features with maximum privacy. We believe that your private spaces should stay private.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require a user account to use the application, and we do not collect your name, email address, or contact information.

2. Image Processing and AI

Our app allows you to upload images of your rooms for AI-driven editing.

  • Transient Processing: Images are used solely for the purpose of performing the requested AI edits.
  • No Storage: We do not store your original or edited images on our servers after the session is complete.
  • No Training: Your images are not used to train our AI models.

3. In-App Purchases

We offer "Pro" features via In-App Purchases. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your credit card details or billing information. We only receive a notification from Apple that a purchase was successful to unlock your features.

4. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect data directly, the app is distributed through the Apple App Store, which may collect diagnostic data or usage statistics as governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Because we do not collect or store user data or images, there is no data to retain or delete from our systems.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store support link.
